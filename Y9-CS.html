Unit 0: Skill Building for a Better Leaner

An introduction unit for Wellington Y9 ICT and CS students to prepare them to be a better learner by equiping them with the right skills:

Unit 1: Cryptography and Cyber Security

An unit on cyphers and encryptions and their applications in cyber security:

  • Encryption and Decryption with Caesar Cypher
  • Cyber Security
  • PKI and its applications on the Internet
  • Implementing cyphers using Python or other languages
  • Information Synthesis
